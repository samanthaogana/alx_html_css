10 Self-Care Tips for Boosting Happiness

Prioritizing my mental well-being is something I'm not good at, unfortunately. However, I have learnt some tips and tricks along the way that help me take care of myself, especially during hard days.

Create a Solid Morning Routine

Routines are effective for people who struggle with self-care. It gives a sense of accomplishment which sets the tone of your day. Finding out what works well for you is the first step to creating a morning routine.

Figuring that out honestly requires patience and lots of trial and error. However, with time, you will easily begin to notice things that make you feel lighter and brighter.
